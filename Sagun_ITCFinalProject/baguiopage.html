BAGUIO CITY

ALL ABOUT VIGAN

DESCRIPTION

Baguio is a mountain town with universities and resorts on the Philippines' Luzon island. It's known as the "City of Pines" and is especially popular in the summer due to the unusually cool weather. Burnham Park, with gardens and a lake, is at its heart. Baguio Cathedral, which was completed in 1936 and has a rose-hued exterior, is close by. Session Road, the main thoroughfare, is lined with shops, restaurants, and entertainment options.
