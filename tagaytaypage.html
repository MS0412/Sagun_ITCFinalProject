TAGAYTAY CITY

ALL ABOUT TAGAYTAY

DESCRIPTION

Tagaytay is a renowned tourist destination on the Philippine island of Luzon, located south of Manila. It is on a ridge above Taal Volcano Island, an active volcano surrounded by Taal Lake, and is known for its warm climate. People's Park in the Sky, which overlooks the neighborhood, is built on the grounds of a never-completed presidential house. Picnic Grove is a hiking and biking trail with a zip line.
